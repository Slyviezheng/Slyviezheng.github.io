About

I am currently pursuing an MSc in Digital Systems Design at the School of Electronics and Computer Science (ECS), University of Southampton, under the supervision of Professor Sajjad Taravati. My master’s project focuses on the design and implementation of neural networks on physical neural networks (PNN) or physics-inspired engines.

My research spans full-stack optimization from algorithm to hardware, including hardware-aware algorithm design, digital system implementation, computer architecture, and AI accelerator architecture.

I received my BEng in Microelectronics Science and Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology in June 2025. Since Winter 2024, I have been collaborating remotely on AI chip research.
